Analytics Overview

2020 - the beginning of the Pandemic. Unprecedented restrictions of movements and lockdowns.
What do most people do when they are stuck at home? According to Nielsen, "The Office" had more than 57 billion minutes streamed in 2020. Why is the show so popular even after nearly a decade from its original airing? In recent years, many podcasts have popped up trying to answer this question. These podcasts track the history of the show and take deep dives into various episodes to figure out the answer
